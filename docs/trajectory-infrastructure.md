# Human trajectory infrastructure

## Thesis

> Humans do not fail primarily from lack of intelligence.  
> They fail from **trajectory drift** under entropy, isolation, weak interaction loops, and broken momentum systems.

**trajectory-native** sustains desired human trajectory under entropy.

Not a journaling app. Not a thinking tool. A **personal trajectory operating system**.

---

## Core shift

| From | To |
|------|-----|
| Reflection | **Intervention** |
| Notes & logs | **Trajectory events** |
| Insights | **Momentum** |
| Solo cognition | **Interaction-mediated stability** |
| reflection → stop | **reflection → action → environment → feedback → recalibration** |

---

## Atomic unit: trajectory events

What happened in reality — not abstract thoughts.

- who the user interacted with
- which actions were avoided
- what generated momentum
- what caused entropy
- what increased energy
- what collapsed execution
- which environments produced alignment
- which interactions restored action

---

## Layers (v0.6 direction)

| Layer | Role |
|-------|------|
| **Trajectory events** | Core data — interactions, momentum, entropy |
| **Momentum engine** | What sustains action; recovery latency; interaction energy |
| **Intervention** | Where drift is detected; what to do next |
| **Interaction intelligence** | Who/what increases or drains momentum |
| **Reality loop** | Close loops in the world, not only in the UI |
