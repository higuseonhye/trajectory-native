## Two layers

| Layer | Audience | Tone |
|-------|----------|------|
| **Steering** | Everyone entering | Emotional, human, tiny turns |
| **Systems** | Operators, founders, teams | Trajectory, compounding, judgment |

Entry is emotional. Depth comes later.
