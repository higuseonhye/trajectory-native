# Case studies

Examples of trajectory-native humans and systems.

**Not celebrity worship.** Focus on observable compounding behavior, ownership construction, and long-range coherence.

---

## Selection criteria

Include cases that demonstrate:
- Compounding behavior over years, not months
- Ownership and asset construction
- Long-range thinking under entropy
- Leverage systems built deliberately
- Enduring systems, not viral moments

Exclude:
- Hype-driven success stories
- Prestige-only narratives
- Labor-only optimization framed as wisdom
- Tool-dependent achievements with no underlying trajectory

---

## Case template

Each case study should cover:

1. **Starting trajectory** — context and constraints
2. **Compounding mechanisms** — what accumulated
3. **Ownership built** — assets, equity, systems, distribution
4. **Leverage types used** — code, media, capital, organizational
5. **Drift avoided** — what entropy sources were managed
6. **Product-relevant signals** — what could be detected or supported by infrastructure
