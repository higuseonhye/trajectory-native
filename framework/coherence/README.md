# Coherence

Long-range alignment across domains — preventing fragmentation while maintaining compounding direction.

---

## Domains of coherence

| Domain | Coherent when… | Drifts when… |
|--------|----------------|--------------|
| **Work** | Projects accumulate toward ownership | Fragmented projects, no leverage |
| **Money** | Allocation toward investment and assets | Consumption, prestige spending |
| **Relationships** | Deep, mutual, compounding | Transactional networking |
| **Family** | Intentional presence and alignment | Workaholism, neglect |
| **Faith / purpose** | Integrity and direction maintained | Speed, status, desire override |
| **Products** | Systems that compound | Feature churn, no institutional memory |
| **Health** | Sustainable energy systems | Burnout cycles |
| **Learning** | Applied toward ownership | Infinite apprentice mode |

---

## Coherence vs optimization

Coherence is not balance for its own sake. It is **trajectory continuity** — ensuring short-term actions do not destroy long-term compounding across domains.

---

## Signals of incoherence

- Goals that contradict each other across domains
- High activity in one domain, decay in others
- Prestige optimization in work, neglect elsewhere
- Constant context switching between unrelated projects
- Stated values diverging from time allocation
