# Principles

Core operating principles for trajectory-native humans and systems.

---

## Pre-tool curiosity

Curiosity that exists before tools, hype, or optimization frameworks. The question is not "what can AI do?" but "what would matter even without AI?"

**Product signal:** Features should survive tool commoditization. If a capability only matters because AI exists, it is likely shallow.

---

## Ownership

Moving from labor-only trajectories toward assets, equity, reusable systems, and compounding infrastructure.

**Diagnostic questions:**
- Does this increase what you own?
- Does this create reusable assets?
- Are you building or renting your trajectory?

---

## Leverage

Output that scales beyond linear time input — through code, media, capital, systems, delegation, or organizational design.

**Diagnostic questions:**
- Does this create leverage or only activity?
- Will this matter at 10x scale?
- Is effort compounding or resetting?

---

## Compounding

Behaviors, assets, and systems that accumulate value over time rather than decay or reset.

**Diagnostic questions:**
- Does this compound?
- What is actually accumulating?
- What would still be valuable in 5 years?

---

## Judgment

Decision quality over decision speed. Preserving rationale, tradeoffs, and institutional memory so judgment improves rather than resets.

**Product connection:** Decision Memory, Decision Journal — see [`../product-mapping/`](../product-mapping/).

---

## Coherence

Long-range alignment across domains — work, money, relationships, health, learning, purpose — without fragmentation or prestige-driven switching.

**Product connection:** Trajectory Graph, drift detection — see [`../coherence/`](../coherence/).

---

## Capital awareness

Understanding labor vs ownership, consumption vs investment, and how time allocation maps to leverage accumulation.

Not investment advice. Behavioral and structural awareness of where trajectory capitalizes vs dissipates.

**See:** [`../capital-native/`](../capital-native/)

---

## Trajectory-native checklist

Before committing time or building product features, ask:

1. Would this matter even without AI?
2. Does this compound?
3. Does this increase ownership?
4. Does this create long-term leverage?
5. Does this preserve trajectory coherence?
