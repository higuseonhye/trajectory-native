# Ownership Layer

Track assets, leverage, equity mindset, and ownership trajectory — not merely tasks completed.

---

## What it tracks

- Assets under construction or owned
- Leverage types accumulating (code, media, capital, systems)
- Labor vs ownership time allocation
- Dependency vs optionality trajectory
- Reusable infrastructure created
- Equity and ownership stakes (self-reported, not financial advice)

---

## What it is NOT

- Task completion dashboard
- Productivity aesthetics
- Busy calendar optimization
- Investment advice or portfolio tracking

---

## Behavioral framing

| Labor signal | Ownership signal |
|--------------|------------------|
| Hours worked | Assets created |
| Tasks completed | Systems built |
| Meetings attended | Relationships deepened |
| Courses consumed | Skills applied to owned work |
| Applications sent | Products shipped |

---

## Product behavior

1. Classify trajectory events as labor vs ownership-building
2. Track asset accumulation over time
3. Surface dependency patterns
4. Connect to capital-native framework concepts
5. Integrate with Drift Radar for labor drift detection
